## Overview

This report analyzes e-commerce transaction data from Target's platform between **September 2016 and October 2018**. The insights cover customer behavior, regional sales performance, logistics efficiency, seller rankings, and payment preferences — providing a data-driven foundation for strategic decision-making.

---

## Time Range

| Parameter | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| Start Date | September 2016 |
| End Date | October 2018 |
| Duration | ~2 Years |

---

## Customer Demographics

- Orders were placed from **27 states** and **4,119 cities**
- Wide geographical distribution highlights a **diverse customer base**
- Underperforming regions represent opportunities for **targeted marketing expansion**

---

## Order Growth Trends

- 📈 Orders grew **exponentially** from 2016 to 2017, reflecting strong customer acquisition
- 🛍️ **November 2017** saw a major spike due to **Black Friday Sales**
- 🎆 **January 2017 & 2018** recorded high orders around **New Year's Eve** promotions
- Holiday-driven campaigns proved highly effective in boosting sales volume

---

## Customer Ordering Behavior

| Time of Day | Order Volume |
|-------------|-------------|
| Afternoon (1–6 PM) | ⭐ Highest |
| Night (7 PM–12 AM) | High |
| Morning / Early Hours | Lower |

> **Insight:** Targeted marketing campaigns during afternoon and night hours can maximize sales impact.

---

## State-Wise Sales Distribution

- 🥇 **SP** — Consistently the highest-performing state (orders, revenue, and average order value)
- 🔴 **RR** — Fewest customers; significant opportunity for market expansion
- States with lower average spending can benefit from **customized promotions** and **product bundling strategies**

---

## Revenue Growth

| Period | Metric |
|--------|--------|
| 2017 → 2018 (Jan–Aug) | **+137% increase** in total payment value |

This strong year-over-year growth reflects the effectiveness of sales strategies and growing customer spending power.

---

## Freight & Logistics

- **SP** recorded the highest total freight cost among all states
- High freight costs in specific states signal potential **supply chain inefficiencies**
- Optimizing logistics in high-cost states can improve **profitability** and **customer satisfaction**

**Top 5 States by Freight Cost:** Analyzed to identify regions requiring supply chain improvements.

---

## Order Delivery Analysis

- Comparison of **actual vs. estimated delivery dates** revealed key insights into delivery efficiency
- Reducing delivery delays is critical to:
  - Enhancing customer satisfaction
  - Building trust in the platform
  - Improving repeat purchase rates

---

## Top-Selling Product Categories

- Certain product categories contributed disproportionately to overall sales revenue
- Category-level analysis supports better **inventory management** and **promotional focus**

## Payment Methods

| Method | Popularity |
|--------|-----------|
| Credit Card | ⭐ Most Popular |
| UPI | Second Most Popular |

> **Insight:** Streamlining checkout for credit card and UPI users reduces friction and improves conversion rates.

---

## Customer Spending

- **Top 5 spenders** contributed significantly to overall platform revenue
- **Lowest spenders** still engaged with the platform, offering a re-engagement opportunity

**Strategy:**
- 🎁 Loyalty programs for high spenders
- 🎯 Tailored offers for low spenders to increase Customer Lifetime Value (CLV)

---

## Key Recommendations

| # | Recommendation | Details |
|---|----------------|---------|
| 1 | 🚚 **Optimize Logistics** | Improve freight efficiency in high-cost states to reduce expenses |
| 2 | 📣 **Targeted Marketing** | Run region-specific and time-targeted campaigns (afternoon/night) |
| 3 | 🌍 **Expand Underperforming Areas** | Invest in states like RR with low orders and few customers |
| 4 | 📦 **Improve Delivery Processes** | Streamline operations to reduce delays and boost satisfaction |
